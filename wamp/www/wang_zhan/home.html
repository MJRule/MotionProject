Introduction:


This 2 week intensive course explores the process of the Film World that starts from conception through to final Post Production. The course provides an awareness of workshop practice, digital graphic design, digital media production, animation, film, video, digital post production, integration of creative IT, engineering, electro-tech, project management, and an installation of a finished work.


The purpose of this course is to design and complete a multidisciplinary work through the collaboration of the different teams towards a completed and functional work. The project will have a defined script (storyline), to follow. Teams will be established on day one.
